How to Buy

1

Create MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive Neuralink.

2

Send ETH to your wallet

You can buy ETH directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, etc. Make sure to use the ERC-20 network when transfering BNB.

3

Connect your wallet to Uniswap

Access your wallet to Uniswap by clicking Connect to a wallet and selecting MetaMask.
